Reverse Audio: Voice Flip

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Reverse Audio: Voice Flip ("the app," "we," "our") is designed around a simple principle: your voice recordings belong to you and stay on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app does and does not collect, how permissions are used, and how purchases are handled. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

2. Information We Collect

Reverse Audio: Voice Flip does not collect personal information and does not collect personally identifiable information (PII). The app does not require an account, does not require login, and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, or any other identifying details in order to use it.

3. How Voice Recordings Are Stored

All voice recordings you create in the app are stored locally, only on your device. Recordings are never uploaded to any server, and the app does not provide or use any cloud synchronization. Your recordings are never transmitted anywhere by the app — they remain under your control, on your device, unless you choose to share them yourself using your device's own sharing features.

4. Permissions

Reverse Audio: Voice Flip requests only the microphone permission, which is used solely to record audio within the app. No recorded audio leaves your device as a result of this permission.

The app does not request or use any of the following:

  • Camera access
  • Photo library access
  • Contacts access
  • Location access
  • Notification permission
  • Bluetooth permission

5. Purchases

In-app purchases within Reverse Audio: Voice Flip are handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit 2. The app may communicate only with Apple's StoreKit services to retrieve product information and validate purchases. Payment information — including card numbers, billing details, or Apple ID credentials — is never available to us. All payment processing is managed directly by Apple in accordance with Apple's own privacy and security practices.

6. Analytics

The app logs a small number of technical events (such as app opens or purchase outcomes) to your device's local console log for diagnostic purposes during development. These logs stay on your device and are never transmitted to any server or third party.

7. Third-Party Services

Reverse Audio: Voice Flip does not integrate any advertising SDKs and does not use any tracking technologies. Because the app does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies, it does not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. The only external service the app may communicate with is Apple's own StoreKit, as described in Section 5. The app does not use crash reporting services.

8. Children's Privacy

Because Reverse Audio: Voice Flip does not collect personal information or personally identifiable information from any user, this applies equally to children. The app does not knowingly collect any data from users of any age, as it simply has no mechanism to collect, store remotely, or transmit user data in the first place.

9. Data Security

Since your voice recordings never leave your device, they are protected by the same security features built into your iOS device, including the device's own storage encryption and access protections. The app does not operate any servers that store your recordings or personal data, which removes the risk of that data being exposed through a remote data breach.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the app's functionality. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how the app handles your information.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@eebconsultancyllc.com.
